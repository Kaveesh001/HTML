Royal Serenity Retreat

Nestled in the heart of Sri Lanka's cultural gem, Kandy, the Serenity Kandy Hotel offers a captivating blend of heritage and modern comfort. This charming hotel boasts 115 meticulously designed rooms, each offering a tranquil escape with panoramic views of Kandy's lush landscapes and the sacred Temple of the Tooth. Guests can indulge in culinary delights at our rooftop restaurant, soak in the serene ambiance of our botanical garden, or rejuvenate in the spa after exploring nearby attractions like the Peradeniya Royal Botanic Gardens and the vibrant Kandy city center. Experience the warmth of Sri Lankan hospitality and immerse yourself in the rich cultural tapestry at Kandy Grand Retreat Hotel, where history and luxury converge seamlessly.

Green Horizons Hotel

Nestled in the enchanting hill town of Ella, Sri Lanka, the Serenity Ella Hotel is a serene haven amidst the lush emerald landscapes. With 105 well-appointed rooms offering breathtaking views of Ella's rolling hills and verdant tea plantations, this hotel is an oasis of comfort and tranquility. Guests can savor delectable Sri Lankan and international cuisine at our on-site restaurant, unwind with a refreshing dip in the infinity pool, and explore nearby attractions like Ella Rock and Ravana Falls. Whether you're a nature enthusiast or seeking relaxation, the Serenity Ella Hotel promises an unforgettable retreat amidst the pristine beauty of Sri Lanka's highlands.

Serenity Coastal Paradise

Situated along the pristine shores of Trincomalee, Sri Lanka, the Serenity Trincomalee is a paradisiacal retreat that encapsulates the beauty of the eastern coast. With 50 elegantly appointed rooms, each offering breathtaking views of the azure Indian Ocean, this resort provides the perfect sanctuary for beach lovers and adventurers alike. Guests can savor fresh seafood delicacies at our beachside restaurant, unwind by the infinity pool, or embark on underwater explorations in the nearby Pigeon Island National Park. Our dedicated team ensures that your stay is a seamless blend of relaxation and adventure, surrounded by the natural wonders of Sri Lanka's east coast. Discover serenity and adventure at the Serenity Trincomalee Resort, where the ocean's embrace awaits.

Jaffna Heritage Haven

Nestled amidst the vibrant and historic city of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, the Serenity Jaffna is a gateway to the northern wonders of the island. With 60 tastefully designed rooms, our hotel offers a comfortable and contemporary retreat for travelers seeking cultural exploration and modern amenities. Guests can savor authentic Jaffna cuisine at our restaurant, relax by the pool, or explore nearby landmarks like the Nallur Kandaswamy Temple and Jaffna Fort. Our warm hospitality, coupled with the rich cultural experiences that Jaffna has to offer, ensures an unforgettable stay. Experience the allure of the north at Jaffna Oasis Hotel, where tradition meets modernity in a vibrant city setting.
